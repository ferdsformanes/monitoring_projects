# How to Create Your First Grafana Dashboard (Beginner Tutorial)

This step-by-step guide walks you through creating your **first Grafana
dashboard**, starting from a blank screen and ending with a saved
dashboard containing your first panel.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 1: Log in to Grafana

-   Open your browser and go to your Grafana URL
-   Log in using your credentials

------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.  Click **Save & test**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 3: Create a New Dashboard

1.  Click the **menu (â˜°)** on the left
2.  Select **Dashboards**
3.  Click **New â†’ New dashboard**
4.  Click **Add visualization**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 4: Create Your First Panel

1.  Select your data source
2.  Choose a visualization type (e.g., **Time series** or **Table**)
3.  Run the default query to generate sample data

You should now see data displayed in the panel preview.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 5: Customize the Panel

-   Change the **panel title**
-   Adjust the **visualization settings**
-   (Optional) Modify units, legends, or colors

Keep it simple---this is your first dashboard.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 6: Save the Dashboard

1.  Click **Save** (top-right)
2.  Enter a dashboard name
3.  Click **Save**

------------------------------------------------------------------------
